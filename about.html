Meet your coach

Hi, I'm Jess

NASM Certified Nutrition Coach, competitive weightlifter, and real food lover. I'm here to teach you how food actually works — and make it feel less complicated than everyone else has made it.

NASM Certified Nutrition Coach

My story

For years I chased results the wrong way — cutting entire food groups, following meal plans I couldn't maintain, and letting the scale dictate my mood. Nothing stuck, and I was exhausted.

Everything shifted when I started competing in weightlifting. Performance demanded that I actually fuel my body — not starve it. I learned to track macros, read labels, and build a relationship with food rooted in curiosity instead of fear. The weight came off. The energy came back. And I finally felt like myself.

That transformation is what drove me to become a NASM Certified Nutrition Coach. I know what it feels like to start late, feel lost, and not know who to trust. And I know what it feels like on the other side — empowered, confident, and free.

What I believe

Behavior change is hard. Mindset matters as much as macros. And no diet works long-term if it doesn't fit your life. I coach from that place — meeting you exactly where you are, with zero judgment and a whole lot of practical education.

Whether your goal is fat loss, building muscle, improving your relationship with food, or just figuring out what the heck is on a nutrition label — I've got you.

Education First

You should understand why every recommendation I make is right for you — not just follow it blindly.

No Foods Off-Limits

Clean eating is a spectrum, not a prison. Balance, flexibility, and joy all belong in a healthy diet.

Honest Accountability

I'm in your corner — which means I'll celebrate your wins and have real conversations when things go sideways.

Longevity Over Quick Fixes

We're building habits for the next 30 years, not just the next 30 days.
